# KDP Publishing Checklist

## Pre-Upload Checklist
- [ ] PDF is exactly 8.5 x 11 inches
- [ ] All fonts embedded
- [ ] No blank pages
- [ ] Puzzle grids are clear and centered
- [ ] Page numbers correct
- [ ] Copyright year is 2025

1. [ ] Log into KDP Dashboard
