Royalty rates for print books and color printing costs changed on June 10, 2025

On June 10, 2025, we changed the royalty rate for books priced below certain list prices (for example, less than 9.99 USD) from 60% to 50%. You may choose to update the list price for your affected print books at any time. Separately, we reduced color printing costs for paperbacks in some marketplaces. Learn more about royalty rate and printing cost changes.

Submissions are open for our 2025 KDP literary prizes!

KDP's independent writing competitions recognize outstanding new books published through KDP. On May 1st 2025 we open this years competitions for English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese and Spanish language entries, and remain open for submissions until August 31st 2025. Participation rules, prizes, and selection processes vary by language. Please refer to our contest pages for specific rules by language of entry. Good luck!
